# 🚨 THE "FU" MARKETING PLAYBOOK 🚨

**Brand:** FU (Dual Meaning: "Fuck You, Slop" & "Fully Unique")  
**Elevator Pitch:** The internet is drowning in AI-generated garbage. **FU** is a weaponized viral platform that detects AI slop, roasts the author with devastating specificity, and turns professional paranoia into a multiplayer game.

---

## 1. WHO IS THE USER? (The 3 Target Personas)

1. **The Cynical Builder ("The Bomber"):** Sick of LinkedIn hustle culture and fake "thought leaders." They want a weapon to publicly or anonymously call out their competitors' ChatGPT-generated garbage.
2. **The Paranoid Influencer ("The Target"):** Secretly uses AI to write their daily posts. Terrified of being exposed as a fraud. Highly susceptible to extortion/ransom mechanics to protect their ego.
3. **The Authentic Creator ("The Egoist"):** Writes their own stuff. Wants to score a `0% FU Meter` so they can post the scorecard to flex that they are "100% Verified Human."

---

## 2. THE SECRET SAUCE: EGO + PARANOIA

We merged two of the most addictive psychological loops on the internet into one product:
*   **The "Spotify Wrapped" Loop (EGO):** People love sharing hyper-specific facts about themselves, even if it's an insult. We assign them a brutal "Archetype" and read them their "Receipts."
*   **The Anonymous Slop Bomb (PARANOIA):** We weaponize curiosity. The target gets an anonymous link, clicks it, panics, and is forced to pay a ransom to hide their shame.

---

## 3. THE EXACT USER FLOWS

### FLOW A: The "Ego Flex" (Top of Funnel Marketing)
*This flow brings in the masses by making the roast highly shareable.*
1. **The Hook:** User lands on `fu.app`. They paste their own LinkedIn post (or a friend's).
2. **The Theater:** The UI shows a loading terminal. *"Extracting text... Scanning 10,000 websites for plagiarism... Hermes LLM is judging you..."*
3. **The Verdict:** The card generates. It doesn't just give a number. It gives:
   *   **The FU Meter:** 85%
   *   **The Archetype:** *"The B2B Philosopher"*
   *   **The Receipts:** *"You used the word 'delve' 4 times. You started 2 paragraphs with 'I am humbled'."*
   *   **The Reverse-Prompt:** *"You probably typed: 'Write a preachy post about B2B sales but make it sound like a deeply personal revelation.'"*
4. **The Share:** The user laughs, downloads the high-contrast PNG card, and posts it on X/LinkedIn: *"I got roasted by FU. At least I'm not a 'Reply Guy'."* Their audience sees the URL and goes to try it themselves.

### FLOW B: The "Slop Bomb" (The Viral Warfare Loop)
*This is the core retention and monetization loop.*
1. **The Hit:** A user hates a competitor's post. They paste it into FU and select **"Drop Anonymous Slop Bomb."**
2. **The Trap:** The app gives them a unique link (`fu.app/roast/xyz`). The bomber drops this link in the target's LinkedIn comments or DMs.
3. **The Dopamine Hit (For the Bomber):** The bomber has a dashboard tracking the link. When the target clicks it, the bomber's screen flashes: **"💥 DAVE JUST READ IT."**
4. **The Panic (For the Target):** The target opens the link. They see their post totally dismantled and exposed as AI plagiarism. 
5. **The Ransom:** Below the roast, a timer is ticking down: 
   > *"This roast is currently Private. In 24:00:00, it will detonate and be published to the Global Wall of Shame. Prove you are human, or pay the $49 De-escalation Ransom to permanently delete this."*
6. **The Payoff:** The target panics and pays $49 to save their reputation (Extortion-as-a-Service).
7. **The Revenge Loop:** After paying, the target is angry. A button appears: *"Don't get mad. Get even. Drop an anonymous Slop Bomb on someone else."* The cycle repeats.

### FLOW C: The Daily Slop Board (Community Retention)
*For the users who just want to watch the world burn.*
1. **The Wall of Shame:** A public leaderboard showing the highest FU Scores of the day.
2. **The Outrage Bounty:** If a user hates an influencer on the board, they can spend 1 Credit ($0.10) to "Up-Roast" them. 
3. **The Fire:** The influencer's bounty pool grows (`🔥 $450 Bounty Pool`). To claim the bounty or clear their name, the influencer must upload a "Human Proof Video" (e.g., writing the post on a whiteboard).

## 4. MARKETING LAUNCH STRATEGY (Day 1)

*   **Hour 1:** Seed the app. Run the latest posts from 5 famous, polarizing LinkedIn "Thought Leaders" through the app. 
*   **Hour 2:** Download those 5 "Spotify Wrapped" style PNG roast cards.
*   **Hour 3:** Post them on X and LinkedIn, tagging the influencers. *"We built an AI to detect fake AI hustle culture. Here are the receipts for @Influencer."*
*   **Hour 4:** Wait for one of them to reply angrily. The algorithm will boost the fight. The link will spread.
*   **Hour 5:** When normal users arrive, they will immediately start checking their own posts (Flow A) or bombing their bosses/competitors (Flow B). 

> **Core Philosophy:** The product is not a plagiarism checker. **The product is social drama.** Sell the dopamine hit of exposing a fraud.
